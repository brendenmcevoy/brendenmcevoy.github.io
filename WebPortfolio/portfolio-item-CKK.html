Corey's Knick KnacksDummy Online Retailer

Shopping made easy!

Corey's Knick Knacks is a dummy online retailer that I used in school to make a retail application. This application has an ASP.NET front end for clients and a business facing UI made with WPF. Employees can add, remove, search, and sort products. Clients can purchase products from the web application.

Made with: C#, WPF, ASP.NET, SQL Server
