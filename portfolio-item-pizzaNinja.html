Pizza NinjaMaintenance Task Manager

Manage your work!

Pizza Ninja is an application I developed for Lucky Slice Pizza. It is intended to help the events crew manage maintenance tasks on specific event trucks. Employees can view and complete tasks. Admins can do employee tasks as well as view and edit Employees, Trucks, and Jobs. I am the sole developer and designer of Pizza Ninja.

Made with: C#, WPF, and SQL Server.
